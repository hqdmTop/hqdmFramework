acceptance_of_offer

A socially_constructed_activity that is the acceptance of an offer as part_of an agree_contract.

EXPRESS specification:

ENTITY acceptance_of_offer
SUBTYPE OF ( socially_constructed_activity);
SELF\activity.references  :  SET [1:1] OF offer;
SELF\socially_constructed_activity.part_of  :  SET [1:1] OF agree_contract;
END_ENTITY;

Attribute definitions:

references  :  A references relationship type where an acceptance_of_offer references exactly one offer that is accepted.
part_of  :  A part_of relationship type where an acceptance_of_offer is part_of just one agree_contract.
